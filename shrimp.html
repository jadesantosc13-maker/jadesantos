what is buttered Seafoods?

Buttered Seafoods is a popular Filipino dish made by cooking a mix of seafood-such as shrimp, crab, squid, mussels, or clams in a rich, flavorful butter and garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo mixed seafoods (shrimp,crab,mussels,squid)
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped(optional)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (to prevent butter from burning
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (optional fot flavor)
  • 1 tablesppon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 pieces red chili (optional, for spicy version)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or calamansi juice
  • Chopped parsley or spring onions(for garnish)
  • 3pcs of corn

Instructions

  1. Prepare the seafoods: Clean and wash the shrimp,crab,mussels, and squid. Drain well.
  2. Boil or steam the seafoods: In a pot, boil water a pinch of salt. Add the seaffods and cook for about 3-5 minutes or until just done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Melt the butter. In a large pan or wok, heat 1 tablespoon cooking oil(to keep the butter from burning). Add the butter and let in melt over medium heat.
  4. Saute the garlic: Add the minced garlic (and onions if desired). Saute until fragrant and golden brown.
  5. Add seasonings: pour in soy sauce,oyster sauce, and sugar. stir well to combine all flavors.
  6. Mix in the seafoods: add the boiled seafoods to the pan. toss gently to coat them evenly with the butter sauce.
  7. Add flavor seasons with salt,pepper, and chili(if you want it spicy). squeeze some lemon or calamansi juice for a tangy taste.
  8. Simmer and serve: let it simmer for about 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. transfer to serving dish and garnish with chopped parsley or spring onions.
